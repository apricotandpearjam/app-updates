Getting started

  • Design a signature. Type your name, role, links and tagline, add a logo, and pick a layout. The live preview shows exactly what it will look like in an email, on light and dark backgrounds.
  • Install into Mac Mail. Click Install to Mac Mail. The first time, SigForge asks you to grant access to your Mail folder (a standard macOS panel). It then writes your signature in and locks it. In Mail, assign the signature to your account under Mail ▸ Settings ▸ Signatures.
  • Use it anywhere else. Click Use in… to copy your signature for Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, Proton, Spark, Fastmail and more, logo included.

Frequently asked

Where are my signatures kept?
Every signature is saved automatically and synced across your Macs with iCloud, and backed up, so you never lose one.

Can I import an existing signature?
Yes. Use Import → From Mail to bring a signature already in Apple Mail into SigForge as editable fields, or paste in HTML.

Install did nothing?
Make sure Apple Mail is set up with at least one account and one signature first, then grant SigForge access to your Mail folder when prompted.
